about me

i'm a very silly goober that you may find on the internet.

i am brazillian, bisexual, black and i use he/they pronouns

i like music, a lot. one of my favorite genres is rap, generally the more underground part of it. i had a huge digicore phase and still listen to a lot of it today (quinn is my most played artist of all time, with over 2 thousand plays), some of my favorites are quinn, twikipedia, jane remover (specially her dariacore stuff) and ericdoa. i also like nettspend and smokingskul a lot.

recently i've been listening to a lot of dean blunt, he usually does hypnagogic pop, art pop and experimental rock n stuff. one thing i like about dean a lot is that he can be very experimental but without being extremely chaotic and loud (even in his noise stuff)

some of my favorite albums of all time are clube da esquina by milton nascimento, twin fantasy (mirror to mirror) by car seat headrest, self-titled by arthur verocai, daughter of darkness by natural snow buildings (the reissue) and prelude au sommeil by jean-jacques perrey.

i've been also trying to get into black metal, specially atmospheric and blackgaze. i think the EP demo three by life/sadness/Damián Antón Ojeda is a 10/10. send me recommendations on albumoftheyear if you got some

mentioned earlier, i absolutely love dariacore. i think the obsessed post-frailty mix is one of the best dj mixes of all time and a 10/10 it is so chaotic and i love it so much for that.

personally, i like open source, but not in the way "i hate non-free software grrr" i mostly use open source when its convenient. i use linux because it's lightweight (without installing random isos and deleting windows defender) and i dont need to crack it i much prefer using a open source alternative than using software with free trials and a billion ads.

i think piracy is #awesome. hrrrr i will take ya treasures ya matey i just downloaded 5 yabujin albums on soulseek and torrented a copy of Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas
